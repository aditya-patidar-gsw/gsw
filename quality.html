Quality Testing

At Gatiman Steel Works, we adhere to rigorous quality standards to ensure our products meet the highest industrial standards. Our quality assurance methods include:

  • Salt spray testing
  • Impact testing
  • Torque tension and friction testing
  • Metallurgical Microscope with Image Analyzer
  • Tensile testing
  • Chemical composition analysis (Spectrometer)
  • Hardness testing
  • Eddy current testing
  • MCD testing
  • Digital profile analysis
  • X-ray analysis of coating thickness
  • Dynamic fatigue testing
